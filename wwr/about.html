White Water Rafting

Whitewater rafting is a type of recreation that involves navigating a non-motorized watercraft down free flowing rivers. Our company organizes trips, provides the watercraft, life jackets, etc., and offers experienced guides to take passengers down the Sacramento river. So join us for a thrilling and safe experience to individuals and groups.

History

We are a family owned bussiness and have been for three generations since we opened our doors back in June of 1965, helping many people make great memories and thrilling experiences.

The Sacramento river has been home to us since we first started in this journey, and continues to be a great location for fun and exciting adventures. We continue to provide an amazing experience for families, friends, company outings, team building field trips birthday parties, and more!
