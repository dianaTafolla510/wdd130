White Water Rafting Trips

Trips to choose from

The trip for groups of 10 people or more has some great discounts. It departs every monday, wednesday, friday, and saturday at 9:00 AM and 12:00 PM. It is great for birthday parties, company outings, family trips, team building excersises and more. One of our expert guides will take you on a wild adventure you will never forget!

The trip for groups of 5 or more, but less than 10 people also has great discounts. It also departs on Mondays, wednesdays, fridays, and saturdays, but it departs at 10:00 AM and 1:00 PM. Smaller groups, families, and friends can enjoy a wonderful raft down the Sacramento river.

The single riders trip is available on the same days as the other two trips, and it has the departure times of 10:00 AM and 1:00 PM. All three trips explore the full river route of the Sacramento river and they are sure to provide loads of fun, gided by our expert team of guides who are ready to take you on an amazing adventure!
